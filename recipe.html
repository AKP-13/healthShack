Mixed Berry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 250ml almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 small banana
  • 1 pitted date
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon ground flax seeds
  • A handful of mixed berries

Blend all the ingredients until you have your desired consistency.

Watermelon and Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredients
